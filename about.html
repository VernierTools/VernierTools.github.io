About

toolbench is a growing collection of small tools for people who make things — designers, musicians, editors, and 3D artists. Each one does a single job well.

Everything runs locally

Your files never leave your device. Images, audio, and everything else are processed in the browser. There's no account and no upload.

Free and open

The site is static and hosted on GitHub Pages. Tools are added continuously — if something's missing, it may already be on the way.

Languages & units

The interface follows your browser's language where a translation exists, and falls back to English otherwise. Units switch between metric and imperial from the header.
